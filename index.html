Pakistani Beverage

Pakola isn't just a drink; it's a taste of tradition, a sip of nostalgia, and a symbol of Pakistani pride. Established in 1950, Pakola was the first soft drink brand in Pakistan and has since become a household name across the nation. With its signature green color and distinct flavor, Pakola has captured the hearts of generations, offering a refreshing experience like no other.

Pakola Ice Cream
Soda Recipe

For a refreshing Pakola-inspired ice cream soda, start by filling a tall glass with ice cubes. In a separate bowl, mix together one cup of vanilla ice cream with two tablespoons of simple syrup or sugar and half a teaspoon of vanilla extract. Stir until the ingredients are well combined. Gently pour soda water over this mixture and stir lightly to maintain the carbonation. Garnish with a maraschino cherry if you like and serve immediately.
